FriMarked

FriMarked is a web-based portal that works as a middleman platform between the Employer (who has a job) and a specialist (who performs a job). In order to access the system, users will have to register themselves. The user can be an employer, specialist, or both according to their needs. The employer can create a task by adding all the required details. The employer can also suggest a particular task to the specialist.

Speech Recognition System

Speech recognition and speech-to-text programs have a number of applications for users with and without disabilities. Speech-to-text has been used to help struggling writers boost their writing productional and to provide alternate access to a computer for individuals with physical impairments.
